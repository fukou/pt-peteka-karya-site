INVESTMENT

PT PKT is open for any collaboration with various parties in any kind of investment, including improving storage capacity, opening new working areas, as well as managing any less optimized assets. PT PKT open to develop and operate its business with reputable local, private and state-owned companies, whereby PT PKT acts as operating partner in the joint venture. PT PKT continue to employ a strategy of controlled growth of its tank terminal-based service network through acquisitions, new buildings and upgrading of existing facilities.
