About us

Our vision is to be the preferred partner in energy logistics.

Peteka Kara Tirta has been active in tank storage logistics since 1972, and is one of the largest independent operators of tank terminals for chemicals, gases and oils worldwide. The company owns and operates 41 terminals in 18 countries with a total storage capacity of 16 million cbm, on five continents ‚Äì in Europe, North America, Latin America, the Middle East, Africa, India, and the Asia-Pacific region.
